◆ DisableForceInside

bool DisableForceInside = true

Disables applying force to particle if it's inside force field shape.

This may help when trying to make force liquid into specific shape.

Force disabling doesn't have hard cutoff, but rather it gradually decreases force to 0 near edge of the shape.

◆ ForceDirection

Vector3 ForceDirection = Vector3.up

Direction for the force. Behaviour depends on Type.

Direction is used as follows, depending on Type:

  • Radial - Unused
  • Directional - Liquid is pushed into specified direction
  • Swirl - Liquid is rotated along specified axis. To reverse rotation, invert direction
