Detailed Description

This component is used to emit Smoke and/or Fuel at specified temperature while having more fine grained control over emission.

Texture Emitters have 3D texture that you can write to, to specify how much smoke/fuel will be emitted, and at what temperature.

You can combine data in 3D texture with SDF, to further fine tune the emission volume.

You need at least one Emitter or Texture Emitter for simulation to have any smoke or fire.
